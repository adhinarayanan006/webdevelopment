More Than Just a Print Shop

Radha Printers & Binders is built on a foundation of family, hard work, and a love for the craft. For over 40 years, we have been the silent partners behind successful thesis submissions, beautiful wedding announcements, and professional business documentation.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the premier printing and binding partner for educational institutions and commercial enterprises, bridging the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern digital innovation.

Our Mission

To deliver impeccable quality and speed in every page we print and bind. We strive to honor our founder's legacy by treating every customer's deadline as our own and every document with the respect it deserves.

The Visionary & Mentor
Late Mr. P.S. Jegadeesan
(The Guiding Light of Radha Printers)

“Printing is not just about ink on paper; it is about preserving knowledge. In 1960, when I started at the Government Co-operative Press, I learned that quality speaks louder than words. It was my privilege to pass this knowledge down, ensuring that the values of honest work and customer respect remain the bedrock of this establishment.”

Founder's Message
Mr. Bhoopathi
(Founder & Managing Director)

"Carrying the torch forward is both a privilege and a responsibility. Having grown up watching my father, Mr. P.S. Jegadeesan, meticulously bind books by hand, I learned early on that there are no shortcuts to excellence.
When I founded Radha Printers & Binders in 1982, my goal was to create a space where this traditional quality met the needs of the community. Today, we have evolved with advanced digital technologies, but our soul remains unchanged. We are still the friendly, reliable partners you can trust with your most important deadlines. I am committed to taking our family legacy to new heights, serving you for generations to come."

Our Story in Ink

1960
The Foundation

Our journey began with Mr. P.S. Jegadeesan at the Government Co-operative Printing Press. It was here that he mastered the technical nuances of the trade. Evenings were spent at home, transforming our living space into a workshop where he bound books by hand—a craft I began learning by his side as a child.

1982
A New Chapter

Driven by a passion for the craft, Mr. Bhoopathi officially established Radha Printers & Binders. What started as a humble family endeavor quickly grew into a trusted local name, known for unmatched reliability.

Today
Continuing the Legacy

We carry this legacy forward with over 40 years of personal experience. While technology has changed, our values haven't. We combine the traditional care passed down through generations with modern digital printing speeds.
